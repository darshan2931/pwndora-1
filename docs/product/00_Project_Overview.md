# CyberPath AI

## Overview

CyberPath AI is an AI-powered Cybersecurity Career Intelligence Platform that helps learners identify their current skill level, discover career paths, understand skill gaps, and receive personalized learning recommendations.

Unlike traditional learning platforms that provide generic roadmaps, CyberPath AI creates adaptive learning journeys based on a user's existing knowledge, career goals, and available learning time.

The platform combines structured cybersecurity knowledge with artificial intelligence to generate explainable career recommendations rather than generic AI responses.

---

# Problem Statement

Cybersecurity learners often struggle to answer fundamental questions:

- Where should I begin?
- Which skills do I already have?
- What skills am I missing?
- What should I learn next?
- Which projects will improve my employability?

Current learning platforms offer static roadmaps that fail to adapt to individual backgrounds.

CyberPath AI solves this by providing personalized, explainable, and goal-oriented learning paths.
