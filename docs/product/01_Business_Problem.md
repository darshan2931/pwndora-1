# Business Problem

## Background

Cybersecurity is one of the fastest-growing industries, but learning paths remain fragmented and confusing.

Learners are overwhelmed by certifications, tools, courses, and community recommendations.

Most platforms provide static content without understanding an individual's background or goals.

---

# Existing Problems

## Information Overload

Learners face thousands of learning resources with no guidance on where to start.

---

## Generic Roadmaps

Existing roadmaps assume every learner starts from the same point.

Example:

Linux
↓

Networking
↓

Python
↓

Nmap
↓

Burp Suite

A learner who already knows Linux and Python wastes time repeating content.

---

## No Skill Gap Analysis

Most platforms cannot answer:

- What do I already know?
- What am I missing?
- What should I prioritize?

---

## Lack of Practical Guidance

Courses rarely recommend projects that demonstrate skills to employers.

---

## Lack of Explainability

Most AI-generated roadmaps fail to explain why a particular skill is important.

---

# Opportunity

An intelligent career platform can personalize cybersecurity learning by combining:

- Existing skills
- Career goals
- Cybersecurity knowledge
- AI explanations

---

# Our Solution

CyberPath AI analyzes a learner's profile and generates an explainable career roadmap that adapts to their current abilities instead of following a generic curriculum.
